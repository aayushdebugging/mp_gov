FAQs & Support
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I pay my property tax?
Go to Nagarpalika → Property Tax → your property is auto-fetched → choose UPI/card/net banking → done.
›
How do I apply for a caste certificate?
Go to Certificates → Caste Certificate → details auto-fill from Samagra → upload Aadhaar → submit. Processing takes 7–15 working days.
›
What is the SOS Women Safety feature?
Press & hold the SOS button 3 seconds to send your GPS location to 3 emergency contacts and the nearest police station simultaneously.
›
Why are multiple Aadhaar IDs in my profile?
If family members registered the same mobile number, all linked IDs appear. Only the authenticated device user can access services in a session.
›
How do I file a Swachh Bharat complaint?
Tap the Swachh Bharat banner → capture photo of the issue → GPS is auto-tagged → fill details → submit. You'll get a complaint ID and SMS updates.
›
